# Capital Scaling

## Objective

Capital scaling analysis evaluates how a strategy behaves when deployed at different account sizes and risk levels.

---

## Areas Evaluated

* Return Stability
* Drawdown Characteristics
* Capital Efficiency
* Risk Exposure
* Position Sizing Impact

---

## Scaling Principles

The objective is to determine whether performance characteristics remain acceptable as capital allocation increases.

Research focuses on maintaining a balance between growth and risk control.

---

## Long-Term Perspective

Capital scaling analysis is intended to support sustainable deployment rather than maximizing short-term returns.

The emphasis is on preserving strategy integrity as capital levels change.
