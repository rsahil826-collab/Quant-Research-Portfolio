# Robustness Testing

## Objective

Robustness testing evaluates how sensitive a strategy is to changes in assumptions, parameters, and market conditions.

---

## Parameter Sensitivity

Strategies are tested across a range of parameter values to determine whether performance remains stable outside the optimal configuration.

The objective is to identify broad performance regions rather than isolated parameter peaks.

---

## Market Regimes

Performance is reviewed across:

* Trending Markets
* Range-Bound Markets
* High Volatility Environments
* Low Volatility Environments

---

## Failure Analysis

Testing includes identification of:

* Extended drawdowns
* Performance degradation
* Adverse market conditions
* Recovery behavior

---

## Philosophy

A strategy is considered robust when small changes do not produce disproportionate changes in performance.
