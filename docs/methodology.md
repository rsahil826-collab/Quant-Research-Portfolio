# Research Methodology

## Objective

The purpose of the research process is to identify systematic trading approaches capable of maintaining positive expectancy while operating within predefined risk constraints.

The research framework prioritizes robustness and repeatability over maximizing historical returns.

---

## Research Process

The strategy development workflow follows a structured process:

1. Hypothesis Generation
2. Initial Backtesting
3. Parameter Evaluation
4. Robustness Testing
5. Risk Analysis
6. Walk-Forward Validation
7. Capital Scaling Assessment

Each stage is designed to reduce the likelihood of overfitting and improve confidence in live deployment.

---

## Design Principles

Research is guided by the following principles:

* Simplicity over complexity
* Robustness over optimization
* Risk-adjusted performance over raw returns
* Long-term consistency over short-term outperformance

---

## Scope

This repository focuses on research methodology and validation frameworks.

Proprietary strategy logic, indicators, parameter values, and execution rules are intentionally excluded.
