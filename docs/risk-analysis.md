# Risk Analysis

## Objective

Risk management is treated as a primary design consideration rather than a secondary optimization step.

---

## Key Metrics

The research process evaluates:

* Maximum Drawdown
* Profit Factor
* Win Rate
* Expectancy
* Recovery Factor
* Drawdown Duration

---

## Drawdown Analysis

Particular attention is given to:

* Magnitude of drawdowns
* Frequency of drawdowns
* Recovery characteristics
* Worst historical periods

---

## Capital Preservation

The primary objective is long-term capital preservation while maintaining positive expectancy.

Risk-adjusted performance is prioritized over maximizing returns.
