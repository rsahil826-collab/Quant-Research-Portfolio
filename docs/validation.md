# Validation Framework

## Purpose

Validation is used to determine whether historical performance is likely to be the result of a genuine market effect rather than random chance or excessive optimization.

---

## Validation Layers

### Multi-Year Backtesting

Strategies are evaluated across multiple market environments to observe behavior under varying conditions.

### Walk-Forward Analysis

Parameters are evaluated across different time periods to assess stability and adaptability.

### Rolling Performance Analysis

Performance is measured across rolling periods to identify consistency and prolonged periods of underperformance.

### Out-of-Sample Evaluation

Strategies are assessed outside the original development period to reduce overfitting risk.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Key considerations include:

* Consistency
* Stability
* Drawdown Control
* Profitability
* Recovery Characteristics

The goal is to identify repeatable behavior rather than isolated periods of strong performance.
