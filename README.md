# Systematic Quantitative Research Framework

# Quantitative Research Portfolio

## Overview

This repository documents the research methodology, validation framework, and risk evaluation process used to assess systematic trading strategies.

The focus is on robustness, repeatability, and risk-adjusted performance rather than individual trade outcomes.

---

## Research Objectives

The objective of the research process is to identify strategies that demonstrate:

* Positive expectancy
* Robust parameter behavior
* Controlled drawdowns
* Consistent long-term performance
* Scalability across capital levels

---

## Validation Framework

Strategies are evaluated using multiple layers of validation:

* Multi-Year Backtesting
* Walk-Forward Analysis
* Parameter Sensitivity Testing
* Drawdown Analysis
* Rolling Performance Analysis
* Capital Scaling Studies

## Research Philosophy

The goal is not to identify the highest historical return.

The objective is to identify strategies capable of maintaining acceptable risk-adjusted performance across changing market conditions.

Research emphasizes:

* Robustness over optimization
* Consistency over short-term outperformance
* Risk-adjusted returns over raw returns
* Capital preservation over aggressive growth
